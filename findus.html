Getting Here

From New York & New Jersey

Take the New Jersey Turnpike South to the Delaware Memorial Bridge. Continue through the toll plaza and stay to the left. Follow the sign that reads South to I-95 & Wilmington/Baltimore. Take the I-95 N to Wilmington. Take Exit 8 in Wilmington, Delaware (US-202 North) towards West Chester and stay left at split on the ramp. Follow US-202 North aproximately a half mile to the Independence Mall on your right. Restauarnt is located on the right hand side of the mall.

From Philadelphia

Take I-95 South to Exit 8 in Wilmington, Delaware (US-202 North) towards West Chester. Follow US-202 North aproximately a half mile to the Independence Mall on your right. Restauarnt is located on the right hand side of the mall.

From Maryland & South

Take I-95 North to Exit 8 in Wilmington, Delaware (US-202 North) towards West Chester and stay left at split on the ramp. Follow US-202 North aproximately a half mile to the Independence Mall on your right. Restauarnt is located on the right hand side of the mall.
